關於雲林智慧防汛 APP
About Yunlin Smart Flood Prevention App

雲林智慧防汛 APP 是您防汛防災的最佳助手！即時掌握最新警戒資訊與各地淹水感測數據，讓您在颱風、豪雨期間迅速了解當前情況。
The Yunlin Smart Flood Prevention App is your best assistant for flood prevention and disaster management! Stay updated with the latest alert information and real-time flood sensor data from various regions, allowing you to quickly understand the current situation during typhoons and heavy rains.

應用整合了重要的中央氣象局氣象資訊、即時影像監控、歷史資料查詢，並提供清晰的累積雨量圖表，協助您有效預防災害、做好萬全準備！
The app integrates essential meteorological information from the Central Weather Bureau, real-time image monitoring, historical data inquiries, and provides clear cumulative rainfall charts to help you effectively prevent disasters and make comprehensive preparations!

主要功能特色：
Key Features:
• 警戒資訊：即時接收最新警戒通知，掌握第一手災害預警。
• Alert Information: Receive the latest alert notifications in real-time, getting first-hand disaster warnings.
• 淹水感測器資訊：查看各地即時淹水感測數據，掌握降雨影響情況。
• Flood Sensor Information: Check real-time flood sensor data from various locations to understand the impact of rainfall.
• 中央氣象局氣象資訊：一站式獲取官方氣象預報與警報資訊。
• Central Weather Bureau Information: Access official weather forecasts and alerts from the Central Weather Bureau in one place.
• 即時影像與歷史資料：查看現場影像及過往災害歷史記錄，協助判斷災情發展。
• Real-Time Images and Historical Data: View live images and historical disaster records to help assess the development of the disaster.
• 累積雨量圖表：視覺化的降雨數據圖表，快速掌握累積降雨量變化。
• Cumulative Rainfall Charts: Visualize rainfall data with clear charts to quickly grasp changes in cumulative rainfall.
